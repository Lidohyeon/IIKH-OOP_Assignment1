Recipe name: Apple Crumble
Recipe Procedure:
Step 1: Peel, core, and slice apples. Toss with sugar and cinnamon, and place in a baking dish.
Step 2: In a separate bowl, mix flour, oats, brown sugar, and cold butter with your fingertips until it resembles coarse breadcrumbs. This is the crumble topping.
Step 3: Sprinkle the crumble topping evenly over the apples.
Step 4: Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes, until the fruit is tender and the topping is golden brown.
Time: 50
Ingredients: apple|4|ea, brown sugar|0.5|cup, all-purpose flour|1|cup, cold butter|0.5|cup, cinnamon|1|tsp, rolled oats|0.5|cup
Grade: C

Recipe name: Pan-Seared Salmon
Recipe Procedure:
Step 1: Pat the salmon fillets completely dry with a paper towel. Season generously with salt and pepper.
Step 2: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat until it shimmers.
Step 3: Carefully place the salmon skin-side down in the hot pan. Press down gently on the top for 10 seconds to prevent curling.
Step 4: Cook for 4-6 minutes, until the skin is very crispy and golden.
Step 5: Flip the salmon and cook for another 2-4 minutes on the other side. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the top before serving.
Time: 12
Ingredients: salmon fillet|2|ea, olive oil|1|tbsp, salt|0|to taste, pepper|0|to taste, lemon|1|ea
Grade: C

Recipe name: Hearty Lentil Soup
Recipe Procedure:
Step 1: In a large pot, sauté chopped onion, carrots, and celery in olive oil until softened.
Step 2: Add minced garlic and spices (cumin, thyme) and cook for another minute until fragrant.
Step 3: Rinse the lentils, then add them to the pot along with the vegetable broth.
Step 4: Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for 25-30 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
Step 5: Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.
Time: 40
Ingredients: brown lentils|1|cup, vegetable broth|4|cups, onion|1|ea, carrots|2|ea, celery|1|stalk, cumin|1|tsp, olive oil|1|tbsp
Grade: C
